Welcome to the REViV Family

We founded our company on the belief that, despite an outward appearance, there is an intrinsic value in everything. We coalesced behind the concept “to revive” because it means “to give life to” or “restore to its former glory.” In other words, REViV is a company that gives second chances, both to broken people and structures because of the intrinsic value within. For that reason, the “I” in REViV is lower case. It’s not a typo! We insist that everyone be constantly reminded that it’s not about us — the work we do is so much bigger than any individual member of the team.
